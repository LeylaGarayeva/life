A personal interview with Dr. Ruja Ignatova

Sep 23, 2016 |

Beginning of September, Saturday, September 10th, to be more specific was a very special date for the OneLife Latin American members. Dr. Ruja Ignatova attended her first Mexican network event, presenting the OneConcept for a full house of nearly 2000 people.

The event proved to be a great success, with a booming popularity in social media from excited future and current members. The OneLife team is always grateful to all of you who share their appreciation, excitement and feedback with us, and we must say that with all positive comments we recieved after the event - we want to give some of the love back by sharing with you one of the most upclose and personal interviews that the Cryptoqueen has ever given.

In this interview (watch HERE), unlike many others, where Dr. Ruja is educating or talking business, you will see Dr. Ruja in a new light that you have not seen before. Mariana Lopez de Waard does an incredible job taking on a different and more personal approach, drawing the big picture of Dr. Ruja's charisma and intelligence in the business context of her revolutionary company.

In a little bit less than 20 min, the interview can serve as motivation for everyone trying to find their way out in the world. Whether from her own personal success story or her vision on shaping the future financial system, everyone watching the interview will find their little bit of inspiration. The driving force of the company that inspired and empowered millions to see and then work TOGETHER for a better future sets her perspective on the importance of having a purpose, bringing value to the world and believing in your self despite all odds."As long as you change people's lives for the better - your product has a value!....Whenever I think about the big picture and the vision, I always think FIRST of the unbanked people and after that I think of business and the merchants who want to do business globally easier and faster..." - Dr. Ruja says while explaining the importance of having cryptocurrency as a legitimate part of the global financial system.

This interview will definitely make you re-define your goals and aim higher, dream bigger and work harder, aside from pinpointing the already obvious benefits of cryptocurrency. Oh, and let's give out another spoiler - you can hear Dr. Ruja's thoughts on being a woman in a still male dominated business world. Let's just say her thoughts on the subject might give a huge boost of confidence to all our OneLife female members (and Mariana Lopez de Waard is just a great ambassador of female empowerment in this industry).

For all it's worth this interview is one of the first ones where you can see a more complete picture of the person behind the revolutionary idea and the leader of example that Dr. Ruja Ignatova really is in her everyday job.

We were all very thrilled to share with all our subscribers and we hope you take out all the positive messages, making you go that extra mile to achieve your goals with perseverance, patience and determination. Stay tuned for some more sneak peaks of behind the scenes during all Dr. Ruja's interviews in Mexico - coming soon!

Yours,

The OneLife Team

P.S. Watch the interview soon in Youku. Stay tuned and check our official communication channels to get the date and link.
